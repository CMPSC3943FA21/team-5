About Us

Camp Quest is a fun and freethinking summer camp for kids aged 8-17. In addition to hiking, playing, and enjoying the great outdoors, campers are encouraged to explore the natural world scientifically, to develop humanistic values and engage in ethical discussions, and to sharpen their critical thinking skills with the goal of promoting healthy skepticism in the next generation.
